Getting Started

The purpose of this application is to generate an "IBM Poem". An IBM Poem consists of 26 (random) words assigned to a corresponding an entry in the dictionary, in this case a letter of the alphabet to form lines.

A three letter title is created. The title will be used to determine the first three words of the first line. The first line is then permuted to produce subsequent lines as per the algorithm.

To get started, simply fill out the inputs and assign words to different corresponding letters. Words are case sensitive and you cannot have duplicate words / phrases. If you are having trouble coming up with words / phrases feel free to use the table and select a peom. Upon Selecting a poem, the input boxes will automatically be populated with words at random.

Furthermore, you can simply re-select the same poem to try and obtain a different variety of words, hit the randomize button to change the combination of existing words in the form.
